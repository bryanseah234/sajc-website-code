Home > Admissions > Direct School Admission (DSA) - 2021 Intake


Thank you for visiting this page. The application for Direct School Admission for 2021 intake is now closed. 

St Andrew's Junior College (SAJC) welcomes Sec 4 (or its equivalent) students who are currently studying in Singapore mainstream schools to apply under Direct School Admission (DSA) for entry to the college in 2021. 


Successful applicants will gain admission into SAJC in 2021 for their JC education as long as they meet the eligibility criteria set by MOE for the two-year Junior College course based on their GCE ’O’ Level examination results.

For more information on DSA-JC, please refer to MOE DSA-JC website.

In particular we strongly encourage those with above average academic results and outstanding talent in the following areas to apply for admission:

Performing Arts
  • Choir
  • Concert Band
  • English Drama
  • Guitar Ensemble

Eligibility Criteria
  • Above average academic record in Secondary 3 Mid-Year and Final Exams
  • Exemplary Conduct Record

Interested applicants who do not meet the criteria may still be invited to apply based on ability and potential.

APPLICATION PROCEDURE
Applicants must complete the application form and send by post the photocopies of the following documents to SAJC General Office:
  • PDF Printout of the online Application Form. (The completed PDF form will be generated by the system once you have clicked on "SUBMIT". Please check your email.)
  • Result slips - Secondary 3 Mid-Year and Final Exams.
  • CCA Records
  • Other supporting documents - certificates of musical exams, certificates of participation in international competitions, etc

Incomplete application forms will not be considered.

Photocopies of the above documents via post MUST reach SAJC General Office by the following dates:
DSA Phase 1:      3 July 2020 - 3pm
DSA Phase 2:      13  Aug 2020 - 3pm

Applicants are to ensure that they meet the deadlines for submission of application forms in order to be eligible for the exercise.

Click here for Online APPLICATION FORM  or SCAN the QR Code below.









Applicants who have submitted applications during Phase 1 but are not offered a place need not submit new applications for Phase 2 as all the applications will automatically be considered for both phases.

Only successful applicants will be informed via email and/or phone.

Successful applicants will NOT be allowed to participate in the Joint Admission Exercise (JAE). You will also NOT be allowed to transfer to another school after the release of the GCE 'O' Level Examination results.

You are expected to honour your commitment to the DSA-JC schools i.e to contribute actively in the CCA throughout your studies in SAJC.


INTERVIEW

In view of the COVID-19 situation, to ensure the safety of the DSA applicants, the college will conduct selection using electronic mode and applicants need not visit our college in person. 

Shortlisted candidates will be contacted by the respective CCA teacher I/Cs.  These students shall receive an email with specific instructions on the date & time of the video conferencing sessions and the expected task(s) to perform.
